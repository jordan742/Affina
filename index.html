Privacy Policy Summary

Affina collects your name, email, profession, and voluntary mental health inputs to provide personalized wellness support. Your mental health data is classified as Sensitive Data under the New Jersey Data Privacy Act (NJDPA, S.B. 332, eff. January 15, 2025) and requires your explicit opt-in consent.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, and port your data. You may withdraw consent at any time. Affina will never sell your data or share it with your employer. You may submit requests to jordan.rabadi@student.shu.edu.

Not a Medical Provider

Affina is a wellness tool only. It is not a licensed therapist or medical provider. Content in the App does not constitute medical advice. In a crisis, call or text 988.

Data Security

We use TLS encryption in transit, secure credential hashing, and access controls to protect your data. We comply with NJDPA, COPPA, and applicable federal privacy law.

Governing Law

This policy is governed by New Jersey law. Disputes are subject to individual binding arbitration. Class action waiver applies. Full policy is available in-app under Profile → Privacy Policy.
